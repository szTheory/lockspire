| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| introspection payload -> JWT signer | Security-sensitive token-state data crosses into cryptographic packaging |
| signer -> active signing key store | Private issuer keys are selected through the repo-owned crypto policy seam |

## STRIDE Threat Register

|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-73-02 | Spoofing | `introspection_jwt.ex` audience binding | mitigate | Bind `aud` to the authenticated direct-client caller identity already proven by the current introspection auth surface per D-19. |
| T-73-03 | Elevation of privilege | signing-key lookup | mitigate | Reuse `fetch_active_signing_key/1` with effective security-profile filtering per D-20 and D-22 instead of adding an introspection-only signing plane. |
</threat_model>
