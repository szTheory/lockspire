- `T-91-11` Release-contract tests could keep checking only older `private_key_jwt` wording and fail to lock the new bounded-reactive support truth in place.
- `T-91-12` Phase closeout could lack a concrete record of the exact automation used to prove the new diagnostics and support contract.

## Mitigations

- Update `docs/supported-surface.md` with one terse canonical statement for bounded reactive rollover support and explicit non-goals.
- Expand `docs/private-key-jwt-host-guide.md` with the remediation sequence, overlap-based rollover guidance, ownership split, and doctor/admin usage notes.
- Add release-contract and integration assertions that pin the new wording and keep the existing rollover recovery proof aligned with the docs.
- Record the exact commands and expected evidence in a phase-local `91-UAT.md`.
</threat_model>
