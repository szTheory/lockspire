|----------|-------------|
| Client -> `VerifyToken` | Untrusted authorization scheme, JWT, and `cnf` claims enter the resource-server pipeline. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-80-01 | Elevation of Privilege | `VerifyToken` | mitigate | Preserve the presented `Authorization` scheme on `%AccessToken{}` so later DPoP-bound requests can reject scheme downgrade from `DPoP` to `Bearer`. |
| T-80-02 | Tampering | `Lockspire.Protocol.MTLSTokenBinding` | mitigate | Centralize `x5t#S256` hashing and equality checks so certificate mismatch logic cannot drift between issuance and consumption paths. |
| T-80-03 | Spoofing | `Lockspire.Protocol.MTLSTokenBinding` | mitigate | Keep all MTLS comparisons behind explicit request-option / extractor seams and avoid hidden global certificate sources. |
</threat_model>
