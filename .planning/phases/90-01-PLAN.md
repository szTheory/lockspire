- Update `docs/supported-surface.md` first so the canonical public contract names the exact shipped slice and removes the stale out-of-scope claim.

- Keep adjacent docs targeted: onboarding links to the new guide, and DCR describes the exact `token_endpoint_auth_method=client_secret_jwt` plus `token_endpoint_auth_signing_alg=HS256` metadata shape without restating a full support matrix.
</threat_model>
